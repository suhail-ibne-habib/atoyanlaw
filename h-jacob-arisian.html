H. Jacob Arisian

H. Jacob Arisian Attorney

H. Jacob Arisian

Attorney

Attorney H. Jacob Arisian has been a member of the State Bar since February of 2013. Before becoming a licensed attorney, he gained valuable experience as a legal assistant in a Subrogation Division of a Workers Compensation Defense Firm, as an intern at the Fresno County Public Defender’s Office, and two Criminal Defense Firms.

Since 2013, Mr. Arisian has continued to represent thousands of clients in various Criminal Defense matters as well as a wide variety of Personal Injury matters ranging from simple to complex Motor Vehicle Accidents, Trucking Accidents, Motorcycle Accidents, Slip & Fall Incidents, Trip & Fall Incidents, Dog Bite Incidents, Premise Liability Cases, and various other forms of Personal Injury matters including representing minors in Minor’s Compromise Cases.

Currently, Mr. Arisian continues to defend clients in various Criminal Defense matters, but primarily practices Personal Injury Law in the County of Los Angeles, while his practice is not limited to the County of Los Angeles. As the Managing Attorney of the Personal Injury Division at Atoyan Law, APC, Mr. Arisian handles most of the Personal Injury cases. While professional and zealous advocacy is Mr. Arisian’s modo, he is courteous to his clients’ individual needs and goals.
