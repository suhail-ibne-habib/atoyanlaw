Our Los Angeles attorneys are dedicated to being your champion both in settlement negotiations and in trials and all proceedings, whether in civil, criminal, or immigration courts.

Cases We Handle

PERSONAL INJURY

Our firm focuses much of its practice on personal injury law, in which injury victims can suffer the physical, emotional, and financial consequences caused by the negligence or misconduct of others. This misconduct can occur in many ways, from human error in car accidents to negligence by public or private property owners. 

Those who cause harm in personal injury cases can range from drivers who violate traffic laws to negligent businesses or governmental agencies. We conduct detailed investigations to determine fault and hold accountable those responsible for your medical expenses, pain and suffering, wage or income loss, emotional trauma, and other relevant damages. 

This generally entails various strategies to tackle insurance company tactics used against you designed to minimize or deny compensation. Our team can meticulously gather and analyze all evidence related to your case, such as medical reports, accident photos, or eyewitness testimonies. 

This forms a strong foundation upon which a compelling case is built. Additionally, we leverage our negotiation skills to push back against low initial settlement offers and are prepared to take the case to court if a fair agreement is not reached. By understanding insurance company strategies, our personal injury lawyers level the playing field in your fight for just compensation. 

IMMIGRATION

Immigration representation at Atoyan Law involves a comprehensive and client-centric approach, guiding individuals and families through the intricacies of immigration law. 

We can assist you in various immigration matters, from family-based and employment-based immigration to deportation defense, citizenship through naturalization, and asylum seeking. Attorney Angela Atoyan is a passionate advocate for immigrants due to her own immigration experience. 

She can help you navigate complex legal procedures, prepare and file necessary documentation, coach you through necessary interviews and hearings, and represent you in immigration court. We aim to alleviate the stress and uncertainty often accompanying immigration issues, providing strategic advice and vigorous representation to protect your rights and help you achieve your immigration goals.

CRIMINAL DEFENSE

We recognize the profound implications of a criminal charge on an individual's life, livelihood, and reputation. Our criminal defense services are designed to be robust and strategic, shielding against misdemeanor and felony charges. 

Our team understands the nuances of the criminal law system and employs aggressive defense strategies to safeguard your rights. We scrutinize the evidence, challenge prosecution claims, negotiate plea deals when appropriate, or represent you in court. 

Our commitment is to ensure the best possible outcome for our clients while upholding the fundamental principle of "innocent until proven guilty."
