About Atoyan Law

Serving You in Los Angeles, Santa Clarita, Glendale & Pasadena, CA

Atoyan Law is a multi-skilled firm serving clients in personal injury and criminal defense in the greater Los Angeles region and immigration clients nationwide and abroad. 

Our firm puts experienced attorneys dedicated to providing you with professional excellence and compassionate support when facing critical legal issues. 

We understand the strain you may be under after being seriously injured or criminally charged, as well as when facing immigration issues, such as seeking asylum, earning citizenship through the naturalization process, or being involved in deportation/removal proceedings. These matters require an attorney who will vigorously uphold your rights and aggressively fight for you in civil, criminal, and immigration courts. 

Angela Atoyan and Jacob Arisian lead our legal team

Angela is an immigrant whose passion for helping others through the complexities of U.S. immigration law stems from her own experience in the immigration system. She brings that understanding to your case, along with professional knowledge and skill in helping you navigate the process.

She can help you secure visas, green cards, citizenship, and the right to remain in this country and address other issues with empathy, care, and proficiency.

Jacob Arisian is an attorney experienced in both personal injury and criminal defense. He has been representing clients since 2013 with personalized and attentive legal service in cases ranging from car accidents to trucking, motorcycle, and premises liability accidents. In injury and wrongful death cases, he aggressively seeks justice for clients. 

Mr. Arisian is also highly experienced in criminal defense, having worked at a law firm devoted to this practice area before joining Atoyan Law. 

Our firm provides legal services to clients in Armenian, Russian, and Spanish. We offer free 20-minute consultations to personal injury clients. 

Call (747) 245-1200 to arrange a consultation with one of our Los Angeles attorneys for personal injury, immigration, or criminal defense, or submit our online contact form today.
