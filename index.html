Los Angeles Personal Injury Lawyers

Also Serving Santa Clarita, Glendale & Pasadena

Injuries in an accident caused by negligence or intentional malice can be life-changing. You can be left with a temporary or permanent disability causing spiraling medical expenses, out-of-pocket costs, and loss of work, not to mention physical pain, emotional trauma, and property loss or damage, such as to your vehicle. 

Trying to resolve this situation through a personal injury claim will generally pit you against the significant corporate interests of an insurance company. This can be frustrating and difficult, especially when trying to physically recover and restore your life. Insurance representatives may appear to be your friends, but they are company employees loyal to the main objective of paying out as little as possible on liability claims. 

Without a savvy and aggressive personal injury lawyer, you may succumb to their tactics, such as undervaluing your claim, trying to blame your injuries on you, or delaying your case to wear you down into accepting an unfair settlement.

At Atoyan Law, we are familiar with all aspects of personal injury and wrongful death claims, including how to fight insurance companies. Our attorneys have handled thousands of cases, know what to expect, and how to counter with a well-prepared and aggressive case for settlement negotiations or a trial, if necessary. 

At Atoyan Law, we are passionate about serving your interests, defending your rights, and taking every legal measure to give you the financial resources you need to restore your life to pre-accident conditions as much as possible. 

Call Atoyan Law at (747) 245-1200 for a free 20-minute consultation with a Los Angeles personal injury attorney, or contact us online. Armenian, Russian, and Spanish language services are available.

      Our firm handles all types of personal injury and wrongful death cases, including traffic accidents involving cars, trucks, motorcycles, and rideshare companies such as Uber and Lyft, as well as premises liability cases, like trip, slip, and fall accidents or dog bites.

      We tenaciously seek the damages you deserve for injuries ranging from whiplash to broken bones and catastrophic injuries that may permanently disable you, such as traumatic brain injury and spinal cord damage. 

      Our team also handles immigration matters, driven by a passion to serve your needs in such matters as removal/deportation, seeking asylum, and becoming a U.S. citizen through naturalization.
